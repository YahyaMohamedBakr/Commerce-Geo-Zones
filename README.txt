== Description ==
Commerce Geo Zones is a powerful WooCommerce plugin that enhances your e-commerce experience by intelligently replacing traditional countries and cities with accurate geographical zones. This plugin leverages the capabilities of artificial intelligence to seamlessly integrate with Google Sheets, allowing you to effortlessly manage and update geo zones. Say goodbye to manual data entry and hello to a more efficient and streamlined process.

= Key Features =
-  Commerce Geo Zones Powered: Commerce Geo Zones automatically replace countries and cities with accurate geographic zones.
- Google Sheets Integration: Manage your geo zone data effortlessly using Google Sheets, ensuring real-time updates and easy collaboration.
- Seamless WooCommerce Integration: Commerce Geo Zones seamlessly integrates with WooCommerce, providing a smooth shopping experience for your customers.
- Dynamic City Selection: The plugin dynamically populates city options based on the selected state, enhancing user experience during checkout.
- User-Friendly Settings: Configure the plugin's settings with ease through the intuitive admin interface.
- Professional Logo: The plugin's AI-generated logo adds a touch of professionalism and uniqueness to your brand.

== Installation ==
1. Upload the `woo-geo-zones` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Go to the Commerce Geo Zones settings page to configure your Google Sheets credentials and enable/disable features.
4. Enjoy the benefits of Commerce Geo Zones on your WooCommerce store!

== Frequently Asked Questions ==
= How do I set up Google Sheets integration? =
1. Go to the Commerce Geo Zones settings page in your WordPress admin panel.
2. Enter your Google Application Name and Google Sheet Id.
3. Upload your Google credentials file.
4. Save your settings.
